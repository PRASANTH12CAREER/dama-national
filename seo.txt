## 3. CONTENT & STRUCTURE BEST PRACTICES (APPLIED)

- **One H1 per page** – Clear, keyword-aware, and user-intent focused.
- **Logical heading hierarchy** – H1 → H2 → H3; section titles use H2 with descriptive, engaging text.
- **Structured content** – Sections use aria-labelledby and IDs where helpful for accessibility and crawlability.
- **Natural language** – Keywords woven into sentences (e.g. “gift recommendations for clients, employees, and partners”) rather than repeated in lists.
- **Clear CTAs** – “Get Gift Ideas & Quote” and “Get Gift Recommendations & Quote” align with search intent and conversion.

---

## 4. ONGOING RECOMMENDATIONS

1. **Add a blog or “Gift Guide” section** – Target long-tail queries like “gift ideas for client appreciation” or “best promotional gifts for events” with dedicated pages.
2. **Use schema markup** – Consider Organization and LocalBusiness (and Product, if you list specific items) to improve rich results.
3. **Image SEO** – Add descriptive file names and alt text (e.g. “corporate gift ideas – branded mugs for clients”) for all product or category images.
4. **Internal linking** – Keep linking from homepage and service pages to Gift Items, and from Gift Items to Branding/Contact with anchor text like “personalized gift ideas” or “get a quote.”
5. **Monitor** – Use Search Console and Analytics to track rankings for “gift ideas,” “gift recommendations,” and “corporate gift ideas” and refine titles/descriptions if needed.

---

## 5. WHAT TO AVOID

- **Keyword stuffing** – Do not repeat the same phrase in every paragraph.
- **Generic meta descriptions** – Keep each page’s description unique and under ~155 characters.
- **Missing or duplicate titles** – Every page should have a unique meta title (50–60 characters ideal).
- **Hidden text or links** – No keyword-only content that users don’t see; keep SEO content visible and useful.
