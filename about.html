About Us

An SME established in 2008—one of Oman's leading trading companies in corporate gifts and printing solutions.

Effective 01.01.2026: Dama National Investment has been renamed to Al Majd Advantages Trad & Cont. Our new name reflects our new identity as we embark on our long-term plans. We are grateful for your continued support and trust.

Company Profile

Al Majd Advantages Trad & Cont is an SME company established in 2008 for the supply of gift items with printing solutions. We are one of the leading trading companies in Oman with extensive experience in corporate and promotional gifts and printing solutions in the service field.

We combine our strong local presence with a worldwide selection of promotional products to give you the best in gifting solutions. Most of our products can be customized with your company logo and message. Gift advice and business gift-giving etiquette guidance is always available.

Our relationships have been forged and backed by our direct connections with the best suppliers, the best technology, and the best service providers in the industry.
